About Us

Here at Global, our goal is to give international-level education and make it as affordable as we can.
Global Education & Training Center was first founded in 2016 and it started small. Over the years, it gradually got bigger and better.
In the following years, Global Kids ( Kindergarten/ pre-school ) and Global SMART International School ( Primary/Secondary/iGCSE ) were founded.
We make school feel like a family experience with open-minded, calm, creative and supportive teachers.
